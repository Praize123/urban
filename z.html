Entrance Examinations


The Entrance exam's into SSS 1&2 and JSS1&2 holds on the saturday before the annual convention. Redeemer's High school does not accept students in terminal classes that is students in JSS3 and SSS3. To acquire the form for the entrance exam kindly come to the school's admin office. The fee is #3500 for all classes


School fees


The Redeemer's High school is both a day and boarding school, so the school fees differs on which plan you have for your ward. Boarding house students are expected to pay boarding house fees whci is 120k for junor school students and 150k for senior school students Boarding house students are also to pay for the School's Church attire and House wear. The rest of the financial part will be on the school fees brochure

School curriculum
